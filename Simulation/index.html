What is FIFA World Cup?

The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious international football (soccer) tournament in the world. It is organized by FIFA (International Federation of Association Football) and brings together national teams from across the globe to compete for the title of World Champions.

How Often is it Hosted?

The FIFA World Cup is held every 4 years. This quadennial tournament has been a tradition since 1930, with only a few exceptions due to world conflicts. It is one of the most-watched sporting events globally, captivating billions of fans worldwide.

About 2026 FIFA World Cup

The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be hosted in North America, with matches taking place across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. This will be historic as it's the first World Cup to be held across three nations. The tournament will feature 48 teams competing for glory, an expansion from the traditional 32 teams.
